About HealthSphere

Our Mission

HealthSphere is dedicated to reducing health misinformation and improving health literacy among rural and urban populations with limited access to quality healthcare education.

What We Offer

  • Health-o-pedia: A comprehensive disease encyclopedia with symptoms, treatments, and prevention methods
  • Health-Buddy: An AI-powered chatbot that provides reliable health information and guidance
  • Trusted Information: All content is medically verified and regularly updated

Our Technology

Responsive HTML5/CSS3 frontend handles the UI and API calls to the backend. Flask + LangChain orchestrate Llama 2 for chat ‚Äî running locally with the quantized GGML binary llama-2-7b-chat.ggmlv3.q4_0 (via ctransformers) for efficient inference and prompt. Retrieval uses HuggingFace sentence-transformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) for embeddings and Pinecone as the vector database for RAG-style context.
